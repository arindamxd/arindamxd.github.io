Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the CameraX app ("CameraX", "we", "us", "our", or "developer") handles data. CameraX is a personal project developed and maintained by Arindam Karmakar, an independent developer based in Bengaluru, India. It is separate from any other professional engagements.

Data Collection

We collect only diagnostic data through trusted third‑party services so we can keep the app stable. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other direct identifiers.

Firebase Crashlytics

CameraX uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports and technical information such as:

  • Data captured: device model, OS version, app version, stack traces, crash timestamp, and similar technical details.
  • Purpose: identify and fix crashes, improve reliability.
  • Data we don’t capture: information such as your name, email, or precise location.

For more information, see Firebase's Privacy and Security Policy.

CameraX itself does not run its own analytics servers and does not sell your data.

Data Usage & Sharing

  • Crash data is used strictly to debug and improve CameraX. These reports are processed by Firebase Crashlytics (Google).
  • We do not use your data to build user profiles for our own marketing.
  • We do not sell your personal data. We do not share data with any other third parties other than this processor unless required by law.

Data Retention

  • Crash reports and related diagnostics are retained by Firebase according to Google’s retention policies.
  • CameraX itself does not keep separate copies of crash logs on its own servers.

International Transfers

Crash data may be processed on Google’s global infrastructure and transferred to countries other than yours. Google applies safeguards appropriate to these transfers. By using CameraX, you consent to such processing.

Children’s Privacy

CameraX is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

User Choices & Controls

System permissions: CameraX requests only the permissions needed for core features (e.g., camera). You can revoke permissions anytime in your device settings, but some features may stop working.

Data Security

We use industry‑standard measures and rely on Google’s platform security for Crashlytics. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

External Links

CameraX may link to third‑party websites or SDK provider policies. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

Third‑Party Services

For details on how Google handles data in the services we use, please see Google’s official privacy resources (including Firebase Crashlytics).

Policy Updates
